Not a typical web design agency

Our approach to web design

We’re a team of specialists, originating from Sheffield, structured around achieving two fundamental goals:

  • Designing a website and brand to enhance your online credibility.
  • Growing your business by bringing more people to your site.

Why combine search engine strategy & web design?

Although user experience and visual design is what we’re all about, if you don’t have any website visitors then you’re business will suffer. When it comes to website traffic, your search engine ranking is critical. For example, 98% of this website’s visits come from searches on google.com – our business fundamentally exists because our website is purposely designed and developed to rank highly in competitive web searches. And, because of this we’re proud in saying we don’t need to pay for any other form of marketing or advertising.

We fundamentally believe that search optimisation and marketing strategy must be carefully considered at all stages of design and development and it’s not something that can be applied successfully as an after-thought.

More team info

  • One brand designer

    Creating premium branding for businesses is a very specialist role and something we don’t use web designers for. Online branding is the foundation of all exceptional websites. We’re used to working with brand-guidelines if your business already has these in place.

  • Two web/UI designers

    Most projects are worked on by two designers with slightly different expertise. Questions?

Can I make changes to the sites myself?

None technical staff should be able to edit every key aspect of a website efficiently without out the fear of breaking it. Our sites will be custom built specifically for you and/or your staff providing full control of whatever you need.

How do I know you can improve my SEO?

If you’re getting help from an SEO business, a good way to tell if they’re worth their salt is to look at how well their own website ranks. If they can’t get their own site to perform, they’ll likely struggle to do the same for you. We’ll use the same methods that helped you find our site, to bring people to yours.

Do you offer a discount for charities?

We love working with not-for-profit charities and ethical organisations. We offer half price web design and development services for registered charities. Get in contact for more info.

Websites can be built cheaply. Why do these sites cost more?

The best way to assess value is to review a companies hourly rate or how long they take building a site. Quality websites take time to build, we don’t speed up development by using templates or relying on plugins, every site we build is completely custom and unique.

Do you offer a PPC or development only service?

We’re happy to assist you through any phase of creating your website. This can include any of these components; strategy, content creation, marketing, design, development and SEO.

How much does a website cost?

We quote specifically depending on your requirements and what is needed to get people to your site. This requires research and a strategy. As a guide to our pricing full projects with an SEO focus start at around £5,000, simple redesigns from around £4,000.

Can we meet in person in Sheffield?

We generally work independently from our own office but have meeting space at our registered address at The Circle in Sheffield. If you’d prefer not to have video calls we can meet you in person.

Will a new site enhance business?

As well as looking great, our websites increase traffic and exposure

It’s a misconception that a newly built website will immediately start bringing in traffic through services like Google. The harsh reality is that a new website often won’t have any visits for the first 1-2 years unless it has been specifically optimised or it targets achievable and popular search terms.

Websites that rank well are designed and developed this way from the beginning, with SEO woven into every aspect of their design, content and structure. SEO companies rarely focus on web design but it’s something we’ve seen that is critical in helping our clients grow their businesses and online presence.
